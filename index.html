Frequently Asked Questions

Our online sessions are conducted live via high-definition video streaming. Each session begins with a brief breathing exercise (pranayama), followed by a guided yoga flow tailored to the class level, and concludes with guided relaxation (savasana). You will receive feedback and adjustments in real-time.
We offer multiple time slots throughout the day to fit different schedules: Morning flows run from 6:30 AM to 8:00 AM, midday sessions from 12:00 PM to 1:00 PM, and evening restorative classes from 6:00 PM to 7:30 PM. Active members can attend any slot.
We offer a wide variety of classes, including Daily Yoga (Hatha & Vinyasa Flow), Face Yoga (for natural rejuvenation and tone), and specialized Women's Wellness programs (focusing on hormonal balance, PCOD/PCOS, and pre/post-natal care).
