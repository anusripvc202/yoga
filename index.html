Frequently Asked Questions

Our online sessions are conducted live via Zoom, using high-definition video streaming for a seamless interactive experience. Each session begins with a brief breathing exercise (pranayama), followed by a guided yoga flow tailored to the class level, and concludes with guided relaxation (savasana). You will receive feedback and adjustments in real time.
We offer multiple time slots throughout the day to fit different schedules: morning sessions run from 6:00 AM to 7:15 AM, and late-morning sessions from 10:00 AM to 11:15 AM. Active members can attend any slot.
We offer a wide variety of classes, including Daily Yoga (Hatha, Ashtanga & Vinyasa Flow), Face Yoga (for natural rejuvenation and tone), and specialized Women's Wellness programs (focusing on hormonal balance, PCOD/PCOS, and pre/post-natal care).
