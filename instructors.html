Kamya is a certified international yoga practitioner, mindfulness coach, and wellness influencer with over 7 years of teaching experience. She has built a thriving community of over 920K+ followers globally by sharing accessible, therapeutic yoga routines.

Her classes focus on breathing techniques (pranayama), functional body alignment, hormonal rejuvenation, and daily mindfulness practices. She believes that yoga is not just a form of exercise, but a lifelong journey towards physical health and emotional stillness.
